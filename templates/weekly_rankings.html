How It Works

The code scrapes the scoreboard table from the League Scoreboard page. It then compares a team's stats for the whole week against the stats for every other team and computes the score. The result of all these comparsions are displayed in the link above for the user to see. Teams with more wins against their peers are ranked higher in the "Weekly Rankings".
